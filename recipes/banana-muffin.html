Banana Muffins

Return to Homepage

Description

These banana muffins are fluffy and delicious. You can make this recipe your own by adding chocolate chips or nuts.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 Tbsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 bananas, mashed
  • 3/4 cups sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 Tbsp vanilla

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Mix together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and cinnamon in a small mixing bowl.
  3. In separate large mixing bowl, combine bananas, eggs, sugar, melted butter and vanilla.
  4. Pour half of the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients, mix and add other half. Mix until well combined.
  5. Pour mixture into muffin pan, filling cups 2/3rds full.
  6. Bake in oven for 25-30 minutes or until gold brown.
  7. Cool completely before serving.
  8. Enjoy!
