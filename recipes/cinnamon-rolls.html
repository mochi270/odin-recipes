Cinnamon Rolls

Return to Homepage

Description

Cinnamon rolls that are out of this world. This recipe is relatively easy to make for the intimidating cinnamon rolls. The effort is worth the delicious treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3 Tbsp unsalted butter softened
  • 1 packet instant yeast
  • 1 egg
  • Filling- 3 Tbsp unsalted butter, 1/3 cup dark brown sugar, 1 Tbsp cinnamon
  • Cream cheese frosting- 4 ounces cream cheese, 2 Tbsp unsalted butter softened, 2/3 cup powdered sugar, 1 tsp vanilla

Directions

  1. Make the dough by mixing flour, sugar and salt together in a bowl.
  2. Combine milk and butter together, and warm mixture in microwave until butter is melted. Whisk in yeast until dissolved.
  3. Pour wet mixture into the dry ingredients and add the egg. Mix with a wooden spatula.
  4. Transfer dough to floured surface. Knead dough for 3-5 minutes.
  5. Place dough in a greased bowl and cover, rest for 10 minutes.
  6. Make the filling, combining butter, brown sugar and cinnamon.
  7. Remove dough from bowl and roll out in 14x8 rectangle. Spread softened butter filling on top.
  8. Roll dough to make a 14 inch log, cut into 10-12 even rolls. Arrange rolls on a greased 9-10 inch round pan.
  9. Cover pan with foil or towel, let rolls rise in a warm environment for 60-90 minutes. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  10. Bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown.
  11. Make icing while rolls cool, pour icing on rolls evenly.
  12. Enjoy!
