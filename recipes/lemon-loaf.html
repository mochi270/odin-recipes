Lemon Loaf

Description

Lemon loaf is a delectable treat to enjoy with your loved ones on a beautiful summer day. This lemon loaf has a rich, spongy texture with flavors of lemon that pull through with each bite.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 Tbsp lemon zest
  • 2 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 2-3 tsp lemon extract
  • 1/2 cup neutral oil
  • 3/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • Lemon syrup- 2 Tbsp fresh lemon juice, 1 1/2 Tbsp sugar
  • Icing- 1/2 cup powdered sugar, 1/2 Tbsp milk, 1 Tbsp fresh lemon juice

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. In a medium mixing bowl, rub sugar and lemon zest for 5 minutes until thoroughly mixed.
  3. Beat in eggs into the lemony sugar. Add lemon juice and lemon extract, along with oil and yogurt. Mix until combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt.
  5. Mix half of the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients and mix. Add other half and mix until combine.
  6. Add mixture to a greased or nonstick loaf pan.
  7. Bake for 50-55 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
  8. During bake, prepare lemon syrup and icing in separate bowls and set aside.
  9. When lemon loaf is all done baking, immediately apply lemon syrup.
  10. Wait until loaf completely cool before applying icing. Take out of pan to make icing process easier.
  11. Enjoy!
